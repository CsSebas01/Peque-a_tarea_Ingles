Written Dialogue

Situation: Two university classmates greet each other and talk about their computer science projects.

Sebastian: Hi Guillermo! How are you?
Guillermo: Hi, Sebastián. I’m fine, thanks. And you?
Sebastián: Very well. Hey, I was working in the computer lab and I used a workstation. It’s a very powerful computer, perfect for advanced graphic design.
Guillermo: Yes, workstations are amazing. I’m testing a computing cluster for a research project. It’s a system made of many connected computers that process a huge amount of data.
Sebastián: That’s so interesting! So you work with a supercomputer, and I work with a professional computer.
Guillermo: Exactly. Each one has its advantages. Well, see you later. Have a good day!
Sebastián: Same to you, see you soon!
